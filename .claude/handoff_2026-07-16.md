## Phase 2 Design Notes

Subagent design notes recommended:

- `FuzzDecoderSequence`: public single-stream decoder sequence target with
  deterministic replay, strict bounds, PCM guard checks, observable-state
  comparison, valid CELT/SILK/hybrid/FEC seeds, and eventual amd64/arm64 nightly
  CI coverage.
- `FuzzOggOpusReaderWriter`: separate schema-driven Writer/Reader target with up
  to three links, six packets per link, derived valid granules, one structured
  mutation, deterministic accepted-result comparison, and one bounded seek per
  input. Suggested file: `oggopus/stream_fuzz_test.go`.
- Ogg target bounds: 128 descriptor bytes, 3 links, 18 packets total, 32-byte
  normal payloads, 20 reads, 8 KiB normal stream, and one 96 KiB continuation
  lane.

Neither Phase 2 target has been adopted into CI. The decoder target's slow
in-flight input must be isolated first; the Ogg target has not been implemented.
